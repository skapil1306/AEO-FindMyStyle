About Us

We are a team of five master’s students, at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. We came from diverse backgrounds but with a common passionate heart in research and design!
MEET THE TEAM

Daniela Marmolejos

Daniela is a designer using human-computer interaction principles and systems thinking to solve problems. She is passionate about working with complex problems and environments. After graduating from Virginia Tech, she was a consultant at PwC for 3 years.  Wanting to solve more user centered problems, she came to CMU’s MHCI progam.  During her free time, she loves to sing (fun fact: she is classically trained!), sketch and explore new cities.

Molly Vierhile

A designer who seeks to enhance tangible human experiences with technology. She settled in San Francisco after finishing undergrad in 2014 and conducted clinical mental health research at Stanford prior to MHCI. An avid traveler, she’s been to 46 countries, and loves photography, hiking, and all things outdoors!
